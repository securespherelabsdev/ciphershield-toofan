What the stages mean

1

Report Received

Your report has been logged and is being assessed by the intelligence team. No action needed from you.

2

Passed to Field Officers

The report scored high enough credibility to be forwarded to law enforcement officers for investigation.

3

Escalated to Specialist Unit

The case has been referred to a specialist unit (e.g. Excise, Cyber Dome) for further action.

4

Closed

The case is closed — either action was taken based on your report, or the investigation found no supporting evidence. Both outcomes are normal.

Status updates may take 24–72 hours to reflect. Not every report progresses through all stages — a report can move directly from assessment to closed.
