How It Works

1

You submit a tip

No name, no phone number, no account required. Just describe what you observed and where.

2

Your identity is deleted

Your IP address, device information, and the exact time of submission are permanently discarded at the moment your report arrives — before anything is stored.

3

Police receive a structured lead

Officers receive confidence-scored intelligence — zero reporter data attached. You can check your report's status using only a one-time code we give you.

What We Never Collect

This is not a policy. It is an architectural guarantee.

  • Your IP address
  • Your device or browser information
  • Your name, phone number, or contact details
  • Your precise location
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • The time you submitted (we round to a 6-hour window)

"Even if compelled by a court order, we have nothing to hand over. This is not a policy. It is an architectural guarantee — the data does not exist at the point of receipt."

Who is this for?

If you are a citizen

Reporting is anonymous by design. There is no account to trace, no phone number to subpoena, no metadata to hand over. You can check your report status using only a one-time code — we do not know who you are.

If you are a law enforcement officer

CipherShield delivers structured, confidence-scored intelligence leads — not raw unverified tips. Each lead includes a credibility score, report type, district, and supporting details. Contact ciphershield@securespherelabs.com to request agency portal access.

Built in the Open

CipherShield is fully open source. Every line of code that handles your report — from the moment it arrives to the moment it reaches law enforcement — is publicly auditable by security researchers, civil society organisations, and the press.
